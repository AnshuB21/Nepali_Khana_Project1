Full of flavour Chatpatey

Easy tasty snack with lot of greens and flavours

Preparation Ingredients Steps Nutrition

Preparation Time & Servings

Preparation Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 5 minutes

Servings: 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 2 cups puffed rice (muri)
  • 1/2 cup boiled and diced potatoes
  • 1/2 cup boiled chickpeas
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onions
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped cucumber
  • 2 tablespoons mustard oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon roasted cumin powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon chaat masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 green chili (finely chopped)
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh coriander
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup crushed instant noodles
  • 2 tablespoons roasted peanuts

Steps

Step 1: In a large mixing bowl, combine puffed rice, boiled potatoes, and chickpeas.


Step 2: Add chopped onions, tomatoes, cucumber, and green chili.


Step 3: Drizzle mustard oil over the mixture and toss well.


Step 4: Sprinkle turmeric, red chili powder, roasted cumin powder, chaat masala, and salt.


Step 5: Add lemon juice and mix everything thoroughly.


Step 6: Just before serving, mix in crushed instant noodles and roasted peanuts for extra crunch.


Step 7: Garnish with chopped coriander and serve immediately.
