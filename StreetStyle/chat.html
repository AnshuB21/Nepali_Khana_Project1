Tasty Chaat

The easy to make and super tasty Chaat recipe

Preparation Ingredients Steps Nutrition

Preparation Time & Servings

Preparation Time: 20 minutes

Cook Time: 10 minutes

Servings: 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 20 crispy papdis (fried dough wafers)
  • 1 cup boiled and diced potatoes
  • 1/2 cup boiled chickpeas
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/4 cup tamarind chutney
  • 1/4 cup mint-coriander chutney
  • 1/2 teaspoon chaat masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon roasted cumin powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onions
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons sev (crispy gram flour noodles)
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Steps

Step 1: Arrange the crispy papdis on a serving plate.


Step 2: Evenly distribute boiled potatoes and chickpeas over the papdis.


Step 3: Drizzle yogurt over the top, followed by tamarind and mint-coriander chutneys.


Step 4: Sprinkle chaat masala, roasted cumin powder, red chili powder, and salt.


Step 5: Add chopped onions and tomatoes for extra flavor.


Step 6: Garnish with sev and fresh cilantro.


Step 7: Serve immediately to enjoy the crispy texture.
