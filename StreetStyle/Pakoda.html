Everyone's favroite Pakoda

Pakoda is a famous snack eaten by almost everyone

Preparation Ingredients Steps Nutrition

Preparation Time & Servings

Preparation Time: 20 minutes

Cook Time: 15 minutes

Servings: 3 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 cup chickpea flour (besan)
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon carom seeds (ajwain)
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped onions
  • 1/2 cup chopped spinach or coriander leaves
  • 1 green chili (finely chopped)
  • 1/2 cup sliced potatoes or cauliflower florets
  • Oil for deep frying

Steps

Step 1: In a bowl, mix chickpea flour, water, turmeric, red chili powder, cumin seeds, carom seeds, salt, and baking soda to make a thick batter.


Step 2: Add chopped onions, spinach, green chilies, and mix well.


Step 3: Heat oil in a deep pan over medium heat.


Step 4: Dip potato slices or cauliflower florets in the batter and coat evenly.


Step 5: Carefully drop the coated pieces into the hot oil and fry until golden brown.


Step 6: Remove with a slotted spoon and drain on paper towels.


Step 7: Serve hot with tamarind chutney or mint chutney.
