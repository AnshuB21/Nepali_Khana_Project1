Chicken Thakalai khana

Nepali food that is served in every home, you favriote food

Preparation Ingredients Steps Nutrition

Preparation Time & Servings

Preparation Time: 45 minutes

Cook Time: 35 minutes

Servings: 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 2 cups steamed rice
  • 1 cup lentil soup (dal)
  • 200g grilled or fried meat (goat, chicken, or pork)
  • 1 cup stir-fried spinach (saag)
  • 1 cup spicy potato curry (aalu tarkari)
  • 1/2 cup fermented mustard greens (gundruk)
  • 1/2 cup radish pickle (mula ko achar)
  • 1/2 cup fried soybean (bhatmas sadeko)
  • 1/2 cup tomato chutney (golbheda ko achar)
  • 1 teaspoon clarified butter (ghee)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Steps

Step 1: Cook the lentils with salt and turmeric until soft, then blend for a smooth consistency.


Step 2: Prepare the aalu tarkari by saut√©ing potatoes with onions, tomatoes, and spices.


Step 3: Stir-fry spinach with garlic, salt, and a little oil.


Step 4: Grill or fry the meat with seasonings of your choice.


Step 5: Prepare gundruk by soaking and stir-frying with mustard oil and spices.


Step 6: Mix fried soybeans with mustard oil, chili, and salt for bhatmas sadeko.


Step 7: Arrange all items on a plate with steamed rice in the center.


Step 8: Drizzle ghee over the lentil soup and serve everything together.
