Egg Bara

The famous Newari dish that is everyone's favroite

Preparation Ingredients Steps Nutrition

Preparation Time & Servings

Preparation Time: 4 hours (including soaking time)

Cook Time: 20 minutes

Servings: 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 cup black gram lentils (Urad dal)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon ginger paste
  • 1 teaspoon garlic paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons oil (for cooking)
  • 1 small onion (finely chopped, optional)
  • 1 green chili (finely chopped, optional)
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Steps

Step 1: Soak black gram lentils in water for at least 4 hours or overnight.


Step 2: Drain and grind the lentils into a smooth batter, adding minimal water. Mix in salt, turmeric, cumin, garlic, and ginger paste.


Step 3: Heat a non-stick pan and add a little oil. Pour a ladleful of batter to form a pancake-like shape.


Step 4: Crack an egg on top of the batter and spread slightly. Add chopped onions and chili if desired.


Step 5: Cook on low-medium heat until golden brown, then flip and cook the other side.


Step 6: Serve hot with achar (pickle) or chutney.
