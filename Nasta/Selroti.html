Selroti

The most celebrated food, served in every festival

Preparation Ingredients Steps Nutrition

Preparation Time & Servings

Preparation Time: 4 hours (including soaking time)

Cook Time: 30 minutes

Servings: 6 servings

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice (soaked for 4 hours)
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup milk (or water)
  • 2 tablespoons ghee (or butter)
  • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • 1 ripe banana (optional, for softness)
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • Oil (for deep frying)

Steps

Step 1: Soak rice in water for at least 4 hours, then drain.


Step 2: Blend the soaked rice with sugar, milk, ghee, cardamom, salt, and banana (if using) into a smooth batter.


Step 3: Let the batter rest for 30 minutes for better texture.


Step 4: Heat oil in a deep pan. Pour the batter in a circular motion to form ring shapes.


Step 5: Fry until golden brown, flipping to cook evenly.


Step 6: Remove from oil and drain excess oil on paper towels. Serve warm.
