Chicken Tikka Masala Recipe

Classic Indian chicken tikka masala cooked to perfection

Preparation Ingredients Steps Nutrition

Preparation Time & Servings

Preparation Time: 35 minutes

Cook Time: 20 minutes

Servings: 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken breast (cubed)
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons tikka masala paste
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 1 can tomato puree
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Steps

Step 1: Marinate chicken in yogurt, tikka masala paste, and salt for 30 minutes.

Step 2: Saut√© cumin seeds, onions, and ginger-garlic paste in Instant Pot until golden.

Step 3: Add tomato puree, spices, and chicken. Stir well.

Step 4: Pressure cook for 7 minutes and let release naturally.

Step 5: Stir in cream and garam masala. Simmer and garnish with cilantro.
