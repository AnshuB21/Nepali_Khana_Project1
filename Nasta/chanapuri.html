Chana Puri

Classic UP style chana puri that just melts in mouth

Preparation Ingredients Steps Nutrition

Preparation Time & Servings

Preparation Time: 20 minutes

Cook Time: 30 minutes

Servings: 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dried chickpeas (soaked overnight)
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon amchur (dry mango powder)
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • 2 cups wheat flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt (for puri)
  • 1/2 cup water (for kneading)
  • Oil for deep frying

Steps

Step 1: Soak chickpeas overnight and pressure cook them until soft.


Step 2: Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds, and saut√© chopped onions until golden brown.


Step 3: Add ginger-garlic paste and cook until aromatic, then add pureed tomatoes and cook until the oil separates.


Step 4: Mix in turmeric, red chili powder, coriander powder, garam masala, amchur, and salt. Stir well.


Step 5: Add the boiled chickpeas, mix well, and simmer for 10 minutes.


Step 6: In a separate bowl, mix wheat flour, salt, and water to form a smooth dough.


Step 7: Divide the dough into small balls and roll them into small discs.


Step 8: Heat oil in a pan and deep fry each puri until golden brown and puffy.


Step 9: Serve the hot puris with the chana masala.
